# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Two overlapping audiences: internal AEROCOMPACT sales staff preparing for customer conversations, and external B2B partners (installers, distributors) doing their own project calculations. Both are technically literate — they know what kWp, wind loads, and KSK mean. They open the tool mid-workflow, want a number fast, and leave. Context is professional: a laptop on a desk, not a phone at a tradeshow.

## Product Purpose

Quick price orientation for solar mounting systems. The user is not getting an official quote — they are answering the question "am I in the right ballpark?" before committing to a conversation or a project. Success means arriving at a credible €/kWp figure in under a minute, with enough context to trust the number (or understand why it's on request).

## Brand Personality

Precise, technical, forward-looking. AEROCOMPACT is an engineering company — the product should feel like it was built by people who care about tolerances and accuracy. Not warm, not playful. Confidence through exactness.

References: Linear and Raycast (dark, keyboard-optimized, developer precision), Figma and Notion (information hierarchy that breathes, not cluttered, productively calm).

## Anti-references

- Comparison portals / consumer solar calculators (cheap, generic, built for conversion not accuracy)
- ERP software (overloaded, every field visible at once, no hierarchy)
- Colorful startup tools (animations as entertainment, personality through decoration)
- Generic SaaS dashboards (could be any product, no character, interchangeable color schemes)

## Design Principles

1. **Precision over decoration** — Every visual element earns its place by communicating data or state. Ornament that doesn't carry information is noise in an engineering tool.
2. **Instant confidence** — The result must feel trustworthy the moment it appears. Structure, labeling, and hierarchy must make the number unambiguous, not exciting.
3. **Expert shorthand** — Users belong here. The interface doesn't explain what kWp is. It speaks to practitioners, not to the curious public.
4. **Progressive disclosure** — The default case is fast. Edge cases (extrapolated values, price-on-request, admin backend) surface only when triggered, never cluttering the main flow.
5. **Tool, not brochure** — AEROCOMPACT's engineering quality is expressed through the tool's precision and correctness, not through promotional moments or decorative brand flourishes.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

No formal WCAG level required (B2B/internal use). Basic operability: keyboard-navigable controls, sufficient contrast for working in typical office or field laptop conditions.
